## Description

Stash is the ultimate personal collection manager. Whether you collect sneakers, watches, vinyl records, trading cards, or anything else — Stash helps you organize, track, and showcase everything you own.

**Organize Your Way**
Create unlimited collections with custom icons and colors. View your items in grid, list, or large photo mode. Sort by name, price, date, rating, or custom order. Filter by brand to find what you need instantly.

**Track Every Detail**
Record purchase price, date, store, condition, brand, model, and personal notes. Attach multiple photos, mark items with tags, and rate each piece in your collection.

**Wishlist & Status Tracking**
Keep track of items you own, want, have sold, or traded. Your wishlist keeps your next finds organized.

**See Where You Shop**
Pin purchase locations on a map and see all your shopping spots at a glance on the dashboard world map.

**Share Your Collection**
Generate beautiful share cards of your items to show off your collection on social media.

**Your Data, Your Control**
All data is stored locally on your device. Export anytime as JSON or CSV. No account required, no cloud dependency.

**Multi-Language Support**
Available in English, 简体中文, 繁體中文, 日本語, and 한국어.

Download Stash and start organizing your collections today.
